You will need to print 14 pair sets of the file "chainlink pair "  for the Y axis chain. You will also print one of the file " Printed chain carriage end.stl" and one of the "Printed chain X Z end Rev2.stl "

The pair consists of two pieces.  All of the pieces with the skinny connecting bottom go to one side of the chain.  All of the pieces with the wider bottom connection piece go to the other side.
